Post on 18 Aug 2018 / Times Of India
Malting barley price negotiations set to commence

Malting barley farmers were made a fixed-price offer of €188/t yesterday (Monday, June 25) – by Boortmalt – for their grain. The increase looks to be a positive sign amidst talks between the Irish Farmers’ Association (IFA) and Boortmalt. The price offered yesterday of €188/t was €10/t above the MATIF price for December. On Monday afternoon, the MATIF stood at €178/t. The increase in price, and increase on the MATIF price, moves away from the current pricing structure. It also seems to be reflective of the new agreement that may be coming down the tracks.
